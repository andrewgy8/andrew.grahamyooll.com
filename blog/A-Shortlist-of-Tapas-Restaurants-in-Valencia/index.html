A Shortlist of Tapas Restaurants in Valencia

Published on 2025-10-12 by Andrew Graham-Yooll

When my family visits us in Valencia, these are the tapas spots we love most. Each one offering its own charm and great food. I highly recommend them to anyone visiting the city.

  • Amor Amargo
  • Bar El Kiosko
  • Barecito Ajuntament
  • Bodega La Rentaora
  • Cafe Lisboa
  • El Refugio
  • Escalones de la Lonja
  • La Senia
  • Taberna La Samorra
  • Trinquete de Pelayo
